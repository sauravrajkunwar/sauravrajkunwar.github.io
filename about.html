Funny Experiences

My wife (then girlfriend), Suzu, and I, during our master's program at the University of Georgia, went to the UGA health center using Google Maps and the campus transit system. After an hour on the bus, we ended up across the street from where we started and finally found the health center.

Life in the USA

Over the years, the love and respect I've received have made my life here smooth and memorable. I adapted well, but I still miss the momo and mountains of Nepal. Sometimes I pretend clouds on the horizon are mountains just to bring a bit of home into my day.

Advice for Others

To anyone from Nepal considering a similar journey: show love and respect to the people around you. It comes back to you, and it makes the adventure worth it.
